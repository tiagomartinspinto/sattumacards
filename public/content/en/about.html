This web game was developed as part of the 2021-2023 Taiteet ja digi project funded by the Finnish Ministry of Education and Culture, in collaboration between Uniarts Helsinki and Aalto University.

The game was designed by Senior University Lecturer Tomi Slotte Dufva from the Aalto University School of Arts, Design and Architecture, together with Professors Mikael Brygger and Tomi Humalisto from the Theatre Academy of Uniarts Helsinki.

The web game's visual identity and functionality were programmed by Tiago Martins Pinto from Aalto University.

The wider project also involved the Universities of Helsinki, Tampere, and Turku, as well as Häme University of Applied Sciences.

In addition to the web game, a batch of physical cardboard game cards has been printed. These can be requested from the authors.
